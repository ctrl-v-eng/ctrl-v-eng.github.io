Welcome to Ctrl-V Engineering

Ctrl-V Engineering is a group bringing Controls Engineering and Embedded Systems to everyone. All our projects are free to use and modify, and they always will be!

Project R

Project R is our first project, a how-to guide for Controls, and a software library of powerful tools for design, analysis, and application. Our how-to guide, Controls Engineering from Scratch is currently under development. Our software library is coming soon.

Controls Engineering from Scratch

Learn about the theory behind the tools used in Controls research and applications today, completely from scratch. This how-to guide and series of video tutorials (coming soon!) will cover all sorts of topics from Set Theory, Linear Algebra, Calculus, Differential Equations, and System and Signal Analysis.

Ctrl-V Software Library

Get some context behind all of the theory and use our tools for quick and easy Design, Modeling, Simulation, System Identification, Analysis, Optimization, In-the-Loop Testing, Validation, and much, much, more. This library is coming soon.
